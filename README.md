# Seduce & Untether
### A Game of Influence

> *"The most dangerous game is the one played with the heart."*

---

## What Is This?

**Seduce & Untether** is a social strategy card game for 2–5 players about desire, power, and the intoxicating danger of wanting someone you shouldn't. Players use Seduction cards to place Tether tokens on each other — gaining Influence — while Untether cards help break free before the web closes in.

It plays in 8 rounds. Win by accumulating the most Influence, or escape entirely with **The Untethered** victory. Lose by getting hopelessly entangled.

The game is designed to be played on a single shared device, passed between players for private turns.

## How to Play (Quick Start)

1. Open `seduce-and-untether.html` in a browser
2. Tap **Begin the Game** and enter 2–5 player names
3. Pass the device between players — each turn is private
4. Play cards, place Tethers, accumulate Influence
5. After 8 rounds, highest Influence wins

## The Cards

### Seduction (Red) — Place Tethers on others
| Card | Effect |
|------|--------|
| 💋 Velvet Tongue | +1 Tether on target |
| 🌹 Slow Burn | +2 Tethers, delayed until next round |
| 🔥 Irresistible Pull | +2 Tethers immediately |
| 🫦 The Forbidden Glance | +1 Tether on ALL other players |
| 🌙 Midnight Confession | +1 Tether + Shield + mutual trait reveal |
| 🌃 Lover's Night Out | Propose a secret rendezvous — accepted: both +2 Influence & Shield; rejected: proposer −1, rejecter +1 |
| 🥀 The Fade | Remove all YOUR tethers on target, gain 1 Influence per tether shed, target gains 1 back from longing |

### Untether (Blue) — Break free
| Card | Effect |
|------|--------|
| 🧊 Cold Shoulder | −1 Tether from any source |
| ✂️ Cut Loose | Clear ALL your Tethers, skip next turn |
| 🛡️ Preemptive Shield | Block the next seduction completely |
| 🧠 Detachment Protocol | Hard to Get — all others gain +1 Tether toward you |
| 🚶 The Alibi | Cancel a card played against you; attacker loses −1 Influence |
| 🛡️ Smoke Screen | Halves all incoming Tethers this round (looks identical to Shield publicly) |

### Special (Gold) — Wildcard plays
| Card | Effect |
|------|--------|
| 🪞 Mirror | Reflects the next Seduction back at its caster |
| 🔗 Entanglement | Split one of YOUR Tethers and pass the halves to two other players |

| 🕵️ Caught You | Accuse 1–2 players of a secret affair — correct accusation costs them dearly |

## Vulnerability Traits

Each player is secretly assigned a trait that makes them mechanically susceptible in a unique way:

| Trait | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| 💬 Craves Validation | Velvet Tongue & Midnight Confession deal +1 extra Tether |
| 🌒 Fears Abandonment | Cold Shoulder & Cut Loose backfire — Tethers tighten instead |
| 👑 Power-Hungry | Immune to seductions from players with less Influence |
| 🕯️ Secretly Lonely | AOE cards deal +1 extra Tether |
| ⚡ Thrill Seeker | Irresistible Pull deals +2 extra Tethers |
| 🌹 Romantic Idealist | Slow Burn deals double Tethers |
| 🎭 Control Freak | 50% chance any direct seduction bounces back onto the seducer |
| ✨ Attention Addict | Loses 1 Influence each round they receive zero Tethers |

Traits are private — only you see yours on your turn. Use **Voyeur's Eye** or **Midnight Confession** to learn others'.

## Special Mechanics

### ⚠ Awkward Mechanics
Three passive rules that punish overconfidence:

- **Overreach** — If your target has more Influence and zero Tethers toward you, your seduction fails entirely
- **Thread Snap** — If a target already has 3 Tethers from you, a 4th attempt snaps one loose instead of adding
- **Audience Effect** — If 2+ players are watching when you seduce, you lose 1 Influence regardless of outcome

### ✦ Status Events (Random, 40% chance from Round 2)
Two dramatic room-wide interruptions that can fire after a successful seduction:

- **"That's Bold."** — Triggers when the highest-scoring player seduces someone. The room is commanded. All watchers gain 1 involuntary Tether toward the leader. Leader gains +1 Influence.
- **"Who is *that*?"** — Triggers when the lowest-scoring player successfully tethers the top scorer. The room recalibrates. All watchers gain 1 Tether toward the underdog. Underdog gains +2 Influence.

Only one status event can fire per seduction. They are mutually exclusive.

### 👁️ Voyeur Gallery
Place secret wagers on other players between turns. Predict whether someone will gain Tethers, lose Tethers, have a secret affair, or stay unscathed. Win double your stake, lose it if wrong. Resolved at the end of each round.

### 💌 Secret Affairs & Rendezvous
- **Secret Affair** — A private proposal between two players. If both accept, they gain mutual Influence but are vulnerable to **Caught You** and **Walk of Shame**
- **Lover's Night Out Rendezvous** — Similar but completely invisible to Caught You and Walk of Shame. The perfect alibi.

## Victory Conditions

| Condition | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| Most Influence after Round 8 | Winner |
| 0 Tethers + 5 Influence at any point | **The Untethered** — instant win |
| 10+ Tethers | **Hopelessly Entangled** — eliminated, highest Influence wins |

## Tips for Your First Game

- **Pass the device carefully** — the handoff screen protects each player's private hand and trait
- **Smoke Screen looks identical to Shield** publicly — use this to bluff
- **The Fade** is criminally underused — shed your own Tethers AND punish the person who placed them
- **Midnight Confession** reveals traits mutually — only use it if you're prepared to be known
- **Secret bets** in the Voyeur Gallery make every other player's turn feel personal even when it's not your go
- **Watch for status events** — the moment "Who is that?" fires mid-game, everything shifts

---

## Design Notes

Seduce & Untether is built around the idea that desire is never one-directional and status is never stable. The rich-get-richer tension of "That's Bold" is balanced by the underdog moment of "Who is that?" — but neither is guaranteed. The game rewards reading people more than playing optimally.

The Vulnerability Traits mean every player has a private weakness that changes how standard cards land on them. Learning those weaknesses — through play, observation, or the right cards — is where the real game lives.
