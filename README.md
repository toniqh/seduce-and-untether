# Seduce & Untether
### A Game of Influence

> *"The most dangerous game is the one played with the heart."*

A social strategy card game for **2–5 players** played over **4, 6, 8, or 10 rounds** on one shared device. Players use Seduction cards to place Tether tokens on each other — gaining Influence — while Untether and Special cards help break free, expose secrets, or reshape the board entirely.

The game rewards reading people more than playing optimally. Every player carries a secret **Vulnerability Trait** that changes how cards land on them. Learning those weaknesses is where the real game lives.

## Victory Conditions

| Condition | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Most Influence** | Highest score after all rounds — standard win |
| **The Untethered** | Reach 0 Tethers + 10 Influence at any point — instant win |
| **Hopelessly Entangled** | 10+ Tethers — you cannot win; highest remaining Influence wins instead |

## The Cards

### 💋 Seduction (Red) — Places Tethers on others

| Card | Effect |
|------|--------|
| 💋 Velvet Tongue | +1 Tether on target. +1 Influence |
| 🌹 Slow Burn | +2 Tethers, delayed until start of your next turn |
| 🔥 Irresistible Pull | +2 Tethers immediately. +2 Influence |
| 🫦 The Forbidden Glance | AOE — +1 Tether on ALL other players |
| 🌙 Midnight Confession | +1 Tether + mutual Shield + mutual trait reveal. +1 Influence |
| 🌃 Lover's Night Out | Pass-device proposal: accepted = both +2 Inf + Shield (invisible to ALL accusations); rejected = proposer −1, rejecter +1 |
| 🥀 The Fade | Remove all YOUR tethers on target → +1 Inf per tether shed → target gains 1 back from longing |
| 💝 Love Bomb | +3 Tethers on target, +3 Influence — but you catch feelings: +1 Tether back on yourself |
| 🍸 The Nightcap | Target discards their highest-value card. No tethers. Nobody suspects a thing. +1 Influence |

### 🧊 Untether (Blue) — Protect yourself or remove Tethers

| Card | Effect |
|------|--------|
| 🧊 Cold Shoulder | −1 Tether from any source |
| ✂️ Cut Loose | Clear ALL Tethers — skip next turn |
| 🛡️ Preemptive Shield | Block next Seduction entirely (looks identical to Smoke Screen publicly) |
| 🧠 Detachment Protocol | Hard to Get — all others gain +1 Tether toward you |
| 🚶 The Alibi | **Auto-intercepts accusations.** Hold it — it fires automatically when Caught You targets you. Full-screen overlay: "The Alibi." Attacker −1 Influence + gains +1 Tether toward you (obsession visible). You gain a Shield |
| 🛡️ Smoke Screen | Halves incoming Tethers (looks identical to full Shield publicly) |

### ✨ Special (Gold) — Wildcards that reshape the board

| Card | Effect |
|------|--------|
| 🪞 Mirror | Reflect the next Seduction back onto its caster (until end of round) |
| 🔗 Entanglement | Split one of your own Tether tokens — pass halves to two different players |
| 🎭 Persona Swap | Swap ALL your Tether tokens with a target |
| 👁️ Voyeur's Eye | Secretly reveal one player's Vulnerability Trait. +1 Influence |
| 🕵️ Caught You | Accuse 1–2 players of a real affair. Correct = accused −2 Inf + Tethers exposed + accuser +2 Inf (but gains +1 Tether toward each accused). Wrong = accuser −2 Inf + gains +1 Tether toward accused. Blocked by Alibi or Plausible Deniability |
| 🎴 Plausible Deniability | Passive trap — hold it. Auto-intercepts Caught You or Walk of Shame. Attacker −2 Influence, no Tether. Your secret holds |
| 👠 Walk of Shame | Expose one player's affair publicly. −2 Influence, affair Tethers go visible. Partner untouched. Blocked by Plausible Deniability |

## Accusation Consequence Matrix

Throwing an accusation is **never free** — win or lose, it always costs the accuser socially.

| Outcome | Influence | Tether |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| Alibi intercepts | Accuser −1 | Accuser +1 toward accused — *obsession exposed* |
| Plausible Deniability intercepts | Accuser −2 | None — pure humiliation, clean |
| Correct accusation | Accused −2 each, Accuser +2 | Accuser +1 toward each accused — *made enemies in victory* |
| Wrong accusation | Accuser −2 | Accuser +1 toward accused — *only entangled you further* |

## Vulnerability Traits

Every player is secretly assigned one at game start. Affects how cards land on them.

| Trait | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| 💬 Craves Validation | Velvet Tongue & Midnight Confession deal +1 extra Tether |
| 🌒 Fears Abandonment | Cold Shoulder & Cut Loose backfire — Tethers tighten |
| 👑 Power-Hungry | Immune to seductions from players with less Influence |
| 🕯️ Secretly Lonely | AOE cards deal +1 extra Tether |
| ⚡ Thrill Seeker | Irresistible Pull deals +2 extra Tethers |
| 🌹 Romantic Idealist | Slow Burn deals double Tethers |
| 🎭 Control Freak | 50% chance any direct seduction bounces back onto seducer |
| ✨ Attention Addict | Loses 1 Influence at end of any round they receive zero Tethers |

## Awkward Mechanics

Three passive rules that punish overreach, obsession, and performing under pressure.

| Rule | Trigger | Effect |
|------|---------|--------|
| 😬 Overreach | Target has more Influence than you AND zero Tethers toward you | Seduction fails entirely |
| 💔 Thread Snap | 3 Tethers from you already on target | 4th attempt snaps one loose instead |
| 👥 Audience Effect | 2+ watchers, from Round 3 onward | You lose 1 Influence regardless of outcome |

---

## Status Events

Dramatic full-screen overlays that fire at 40% probability from Round 2 onward after successful seductions.

| Event | Trigger | Effect |
|-------|---------|--------|
| **That's Bold.** | Top scorer seduces someone | All watchers +1 Tether toward leader. Leader +1 Influence |
| **Who is that?** | Bottom scorer tethers the top scorer | All watchers +1 Tether toward underdog. Underdog +2 Influence |
